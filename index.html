About

Hello good people, I am Sadri, a doctoral student in Computer Science at Kennesaw State University (KSU). I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant and my research focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Brain Computer Interface (BCI) , two fields at the intersection of human cognition and machine intelligence. During my undergrad, I tried to explore the domain related to AI and Healthcare systems.

Before joining KSU, I served as a full-time Lecturer (undergraduate section) in the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Dept. at the University of Creative Technology Chittagong. I have conducted courses of the undergrad students for the Spring and Fall semesters, also guided them with their final-year project works. I think this experience broadened my ability to discuss technical ideas clearly and work in a collaborative team.

Prior to that, I have completed my graduation in June, 2024 from the Electrical & Electronic Engineering Dept. at Islamic University of Technology (IUT, OIC ),Bangladesh. I have published IEEE conference papers related to Arrhythmia classification and Colorectal cancer diagnosis from histopathological images using Deep Learning models. I am motivated to build intelligent systems that have real world impact in biomedical and linguistic domains.
